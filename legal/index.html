Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1st July 2026

Clanker respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is stored, how it is used, and how analytics are handled.

What We Store

  • Server IDs
  • User IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Bot-related data such as balances, settings and statistics

We do not collect personal information such as real names, passwords, or private messages.

How Data Is Used

Data is used only for bot functionality including commands, economy systems, server configuration, and statistics.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our website. This includes information such as page views, session duration, device type, browser type, and general usage patterns.

This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymised form and is used only to improve the Clanker website.

Google Analytics may use cookies. These cookies do not directly identify individual users.

You can view Google's privacy policy here: Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except where required by law.

Data Removal

You can request deletion of your data by:

  • Removing Clanker from your Discord server
  • Contacting support through our Discord server
  • Using /forgetme if available
